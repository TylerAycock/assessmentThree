Cardiff By the Sea

Welcome to one of the most scenic neighborhoods in all of San Diego. Tucked between Del Mar and Encinitas, this small beach town is where I am lucky enough to call home.

A stones throw from the beach we have amazing ocean views. In addition we have a variety of local resuarants. I've listed a few of my favorites below. Having trouble picking which spot to visit first? Just click the button and let me decide!

The Shanty

A locals favorite, this simple dive bar has plenty of cold beer and cheap bites

Best Pizza

Like the name suggests, they definitely have the best pizza in town!

The Sicilian

A great date night spot, with fantastic pasta dishes

Seaside Market

Our local grocery store that has amazing and cost effective premade meals. Great for grab n go before you hit the beach

Fish 101

Local purveyor of all things fish. I'd highly recommend the tacos
